  • Monthly Expenses: Shows expense total with red gradient and trend indicator
  • Transfers: Shows transfer total with standard styling and trend indicator

Trend Calculation:

  • Compares current period with previous period (e.g., current month vs previous month)
  • Shows percentage change with appropriate arrow icon
  • Uses color coding to indicate positive (green) or negative (red) trends
